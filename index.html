About Bitu

Recruitment process

  • STEP 1 CV & Video
  • STEP 2 Interview
  • STEP 3 Trial class
  • STEP 4 Training
  • STEP 5 One month probation

Candidates send Bitu a CV which summarizes their professional history, education, skills and achievements and a Video which lasts for 2-3 minutes introducing yourself. Kindly send your CV and Video to this email recruitment.bitu@gmail.com if you are interested in the position.

This interview provides an opportunity for both potential moderator and Bitu to decide if the individual's skills and character align with the company's needs and culture.

After passing the resume selection, the BITU team will arrange 2 trial classes for candidates. The first one is beginner students and finally, intermediate students. Each class lasts for 30 minutes and will be conducted on the Google Meet platform.
Candidates will receive all the necessary documents to make sure that they know how a BITU class is operated. The documents include a lesson format, a topic and a sample video for candidates to prepare for the trial class.
One observer from BITU will join your class at the same time to judge candidates' performance and give them feedback.
Training period depends on candidates’ performances. Normally it takes from 2 to 3 classes. Those classes are conducted on Google Meet as well.
After the training process, if the BITU team sees the improvement in performance of candidates and at the same time the candidates meet all the requirements as well, you are eligible for officially working as a moderator of BITU.

After finishing the two trial classes, candidates will enter a training session with one of our teacher managers. This step will help you familiarize with how Bitu’s classes operate and guide future moderators with practical methods on conducting a class efficiently.

In the first month working as an official moderator of BITU, candidates must follow all the instructions and rules provided from BITU. The candidates are also paid the rate during this time.
Classes that are conducted by candidates in the probationary month will be supervised and listened to randomly by the BITU team. If the candidates fail to meet the criteria or break any rules, BITU will terminate your working service immediately.

Q&A

1

Is it okay if I have no experience in teaching English before?
Even when you have no experience in teaching English before, don’t worry because you will be properly trained and provided all necessary teaching materials before starting conduct class
2

What should I prepare in advanced for each class?
You don’t have to prepare much because the outlines for the topics are shown in each class. All you need to do is make sure that your internet connection works well during the class. Besides, you can have access to the outlines in advanced in case you want to be super well-prepared
3

Will I get a bonus if I perform excellently in a month?
Of course you will be handsomely rewarded for an excellent performance in a month.
4

Is it necessary to always open my camera during the class?
Yes. All classes in Bitu are video classes. It helps promote the interaction between student and teacher. Unless your internet connection isn’t stable enough, kindly always open the camera in classes.
5

Does Bitu have any monthly virtual meetings?
All moderators are welcomed to join the virtual meeting which is organized every month to share their difficulties handling classes, figure out the solutions to solve them and come up with new ideas
6

What should I do if students ask me questions unrelated to the lesson or about Bitu’s policy?

Moderators aren’t allowed to answer any questions which aren’t related to the lesson. Things such as Bitu’s policy must be discussed between students and Bitu staff
7

What makes Bitu different from the other teaching platforms?
Bitu is a unique mobile application which helps connect students and moderators easily. In Bitu, moderators are given the most favorable working environment where your ideas and suggestions are heard and respected. Bitu welcomes moderators of all ages who are good at English speaking skills and gives them a proper training program. In Bitu, both students and moderators are encouraged to have fun while teaching and learning.
